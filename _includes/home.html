WE LOVE BENTOS AND GAMES

We are just a team of people dreaming about both bentos and developing video games. We formed our team at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa to create a video game in which we saw potential in. We did our very best to create the video game and we do hope you will enjoy it as much as we made it.
Plato, a ball of Play-Doh, was formed when a little girl mushed and molded a wide range of colored Play-Doh. She then left him aside, which made him lose his color. Join Plato in meeting and rescuing other toys, in his quest to find happiness and color in his life.
