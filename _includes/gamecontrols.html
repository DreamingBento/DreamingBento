Controls

Basic

Left arrow key: Move left
Right arrow key: Move right
Enter: Activate

Circle Mode

"A" to activate.
Basic form of movement. Roll left and right to travel.

Box Mode

"S" to activate.
Form of defense and activation. Allows you block and stun enemies and activate certain switches.

Stick Mode

"D" to activate.
Advance form of movement. Allows you to stretch into higher places or tiny crevices.
Up, Left, Right to stretch in the direction.
