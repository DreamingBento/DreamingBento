The Awards

  • Best Opening/Closing Cinematics
  • Best Visuals (Graphics)
  • Best Sound
  • Best Technical Achievement
  • Best Overall Game/Gameplay

The Verdict

We were able to showcase our videogame to the class, and a panel of judges. There was a lot of well made videogames that were showcased, but the judges still had to make their decisions, as there was only five awards to hand out.

Ultimately, out of a class that consisted of give or take 12 teams, our team won two out of the five awards. We were awarded with Best Technical Achivement and Best Overall Game/Gameplay.
